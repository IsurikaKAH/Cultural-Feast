Menu 4

Starter

Appetizer:

Samosas: Deep-fried pastries filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat or lentils.
Date and Cashew Rolls: A sweet and savory appetizer made with dates and cashews, often seasoned with spices.

Soup:
Mullaguthanni Soup: Coconut milk based soup made with a combination of accompaniments to create an authentic Sri Lankan curry flavor.
Rasam: A tangy and spicy tomato and tamarind soup with aromatic spices.

Salad:
Kachumber Salad: A fresh and crisp salad made with cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, and spices.
Cucumber Raita: A cooling yogurt-based salad with cucumbers and mint.

Main Course

Elu Mas Themparadu(Boneless): Marinated Mutton sauteed to perfection with onions and capsicum.
Polos Curry:Polos curry or polos ambula is a traditional Sri Lankan baby jackfruit curry.
Palmyrah Root Sambol: A unique and authentic Jaffna dish, this sambol adds a distinctive touch to your meal.
Lamprais: The traditional Dutch lamrais is a portion wrapped warm in a banana leaf with flavorful accompaniments prepared from an age old Dutch recipe.

Side Dishes:
Carrot Sambol: Grated Carrot, Coconut, Onion, Green Chilli, Lime
Eggplant Curry: Eggplant cooked in a rich, spiced tomato gravy.
Papadam: A thin, crispy Indian flatbread or wafer.


Dessert

Thala Keral: Wrap up your Jaffna Spice Fiesta with Thala Keral, a sweet delicacy that captures the essence of Jaffna's culinary heritage
Kithul Curd Cake: So Curd cake assembled on top of ginger biscuit & is topped with Pani Kaju & kithul Treacle


Beverage

Lime Juice: Quench your thirst with the refreshing lime taste
Goraka Juice: Packed with health benefits.
Cinnamon Red Iced Tea: Steep red tea leaves and cinnamon sticks in the sun for a refreshing tea. Sweeten with simple syrup instead of sugar.
