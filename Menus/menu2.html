Menu 2

Starter

Appetizer:

Ulundu Vadai: Deep-fried savory donuts made from black gram (urad dal) and spices.
Murukku: A crunchy, spiral-shaped snack made from rice flour and spices.
Mutton Samosa: Samosas filled with spiced mutton.

Soup:
Mung Bean Soup: A light soup made from mung beans, coconut milk, and mild spices.
Paruppu Rasam: A lentil and tamarind soup with a blend of aromatic spices.
Shorba: A fragrant and spiced soup with meat, lentils, and herbs.

Salad:
Kiri Kos Mallum: A coconut and cabbage salad with lime and chili.
Kosambari: A salad made from soaked and seasoned lentils and vegetables.
Tomato and Cucumber Salad: A simple salad with tomatoes, cucumbers, and a zesty dressing.

Main Course

Lamb Curry: A rich and flavorful lamb curry with coconut milk and spices.
Egg Curry: Eggs cooked in a spiced tomato gravy.
Kofta Biryani: Fragrant biryani rice served with spiced meatballs.

Side Dishes:
Brinjal Moju: Sweet and sour eggplant pickle
Avial: A mixed vegetable curry in a coconut and yogurt sauce.
Daal Tadka: Tempered lentils with spices and herbs.


Dessert

Bibikkan: A Sri Lankan coconut and treacle cake.
Semiya Payasam: A sweet vermicelli pudding flavored with cardamom and saffron.
Falooda Ice Cream: A dessert drink with vermicelli, rose syrup, ice cream, and basil seeds.


Beverage

Wood Apple Juice: A unique and tangy fruit juice.
Madras Coffee: Strong filter coffee with milk and sugar.
Rose Sherbet: A sweet and aromatic rose-flavored drink


This multi-course meal combines the unique flavors and culinary traditions of Sinhala, Tamil, and Muslim cuisines for a diverse and memorable dining experience.
