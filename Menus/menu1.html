Menu 1

Starter

Appetizer:

Samosas: Deep-fried pastries filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat or lentils.
Date and Cashew Rolls: A sweet and savory appetizer made with dates and cashews, often seasoned with spices.

Soup:
Lentil Soup: A hearty lentil-based soup with spices like turmeric and curry leaves.
Rasam: A tangy and spicy tomato and tamarind soup with aromatic spices.
Chicken Mulligatawny Soup: A flavorful soup with chicken, lentils, and a blend of spices.

Salad:
Kachumber Salad: A fresh and crisp salad made with cucumbers, tomatoes, onions, and spices.
Cucumber Raita: A cooling yogurt-based salad with cucumbers and mint.

Main Course

Fish Ambul Thiyal: A sour and spicy fish curry made with tamarind, Goraka (Malabar tamarind), and aromatic spices.
Chicken Chettinad: A flavorful and spicy chicken curry with a blend of South Indian spices.
Biryani: A fragrant and rich rice dish with marinated meat, aromatic spices, and saffron.

Side Dishes:
Pol Sambol: A spicy coconut condiment.
Eggplant Curry: Eggplant cooked in a rich, spiced tomato gravy.
Dalcha: A lentil and vegetable stew with a flavorful sauce.


Dessert

Watalappan: A custard-like dessert made with jaggery, coconut milk, and spices.
Mysore Pak: A sweet and rich Indian dessert made from ghee, chickpea flour, and sugar.
Kiri Pani: A creamy rice pudding often flavored with cardamom and rose water.


Beverage

King Coconut Water: A refreshing and hydrating drink from young green coconuts.
Masala Chai: A spiced tea made with black tea leaves, milk, and aromatic spices.
Falooda: A sweet and creamy drink made with rose syrup, vermicelli, and basil seeds.


This multi-course meal combines the unique flavors and culinary traditions of Sinhala, Tamil, and Muslim cuisines for a diverse and memorable dining experience.
